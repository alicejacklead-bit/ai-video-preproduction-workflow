# Plan Demo

## Project Information

- Duration: 5 seconds
- Aspect Ratio: 4:3
- Format: reusable series outro
- Series Title: 15S FRAME BREAK
- Chinese Subtitle: 15秒破帧行动
- Signature Credit: MADE BY ANYFAST
- Style: high-quality anime sci-fi action, 3D anime-character cinematic finish
- Main Subject: female elf rider on future sport motorcycle
- Main Scene: one dark subway tunnel with blue-violet neon and digital pixel barrier

## One-Sentence Concept

A silver-haired elf rider charges straight out of a dark neon subway tunnel, shatters a transparent digital frame barrier with her deep-blue future bike, then the blue-violet pixel debris is pulled back by her slipstream and reassembles into the final series logo.

## Signature Closing Action

The memorable reusable ending move is:

The heroine does not stop at the frame edge. She physically breaks a nearly invisible digital picture-frame barrier with the front of her bike, blasts past the camera, and her wake pulls the shattered frame pixels back into the center where they reform into the brand logo.

This gives the series a repeatable identity similar to a logo-break action, but tied directly to speed, impact, and “breaking the frame.”

## Timeline

### 0.0-2.0 Seconds | High-Speed Approach

Visual: a dark subway tunnel seen from a low frontal angle; a deep-blue future sport motorcycle approaches from far distance on the center axis, growing rapidly in size
Action: the heroine rides directly toward the lens at extreme speed; tunnel neon streaks stretch into long blue-violet lines; the headlight and cyan bike accents intensify as she closes in
Character Read: silver-white high ponytail streaming backward, pointed elf ears, glowing purple eyes briefly readable
Audio Intent: engine rising from distant rumble to aggressive near-field roar, air-slicing sound, tunnel reverb
Audience Information: this is a speed-driven world; the rider is the core icon; something is about to collide with the screen itself

### 2.0-3.0 Seconds | Frame-Break Impact

Visual: a nearly invisible transparent digital border exists in front of the camera plane, visible only as a subtle pixel mesh and faint blue-violet edge glow
Action: the bike slams through this digital barrier at center frame; the barrier fractures into square pixel shards and luminous data particles instead of glass; the motorcycle then sweeps past camera toward the right side, leaving a strong cyan slipstream
Effect Rule: the broken pieces must clearly read as fragments of a digital screen border, not physical glass
Audio Intent: short low-frequency hit, digital crack-shatter, passing rush
Audience Information: the series identity is literally about breaking the frame

### 3.0-5.0 Seconds | Logo Reassembly and Lock

Visual: shattered blue-violet pixel fragments hang for a fraction of a second in the dark field, then are sucked inward by the bike wake
Action Order:
- first, a small `15S` forms
- next, `FRAME BREAK` unfolds from center outward as the largest title
- then, `15秒破帧行动` appears beneath
- finally, `MADE BY ANYFAST` resolves at the bottom
- after the text is stable, a simple purple elf-eye emblem ignites once in the center
- a brief scan-light travels across the eye
- all elements hold cleanly for the final 0.5 seconds
Audio Intent: electronic particle suction, clean logo resolve hit, short scan chirp
Audience Information: every fragment mattered; the impact creates the identity, then the logo finishes in a premium brand lockup

## Visual Hook

The strongest hook is not only the incoming bike, but the idea that the rider crashes through the viewer’s digital frame and turns that destroyed frame into the logo itself.

## Output Goal

Deliver a premium, repeatable 5-second branded outro for anime sci-fi, chase, battle, and high-dynamic AI videos without looking like a game intro, HUD, or stock glitch template.
