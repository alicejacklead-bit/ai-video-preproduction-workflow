# Topic Decision

## Topic Name

Elf Courier Tunnel Chase

## One-Sentence Concept

A silver-haired elf courier on a deep-blue future bike outruns a masked rider in a graffiti-covered subway tunnel and escapes by pixel-shifting through the narrow side gap of an oncoming train before trapping him behind a falling safety gate.

## Video Type

Narrative short / sci-fi action chase

## Content Goal

Create a visually intense but spatially readable 15-second chase short with a clear hook, one memorable reversal, and stable character and vehicle continuity.

## Target Audience

Douyin viewers who like anime action, cyber sci-fi, and high-intensity short-form visual storytelling.

## Platform

Douyin

## Duration

15 seconds

## Aspect Ratio

4:3

## First 2-Second Hook

A low tracking shot shows the female elf courier racing through a dark subway tunnel while a black-clad rider closes in behind her; an oncoming train suddenly appears ahead and she does not slow down.

## Main Conflict

The courier is being rapidly caught in a single tunnel with almost no space to escape.

## Ending or Reversal

She and her bike briefly pixelate and skim through the narrow gap beside the train; the pursuer cannot follow, and she seals him off with a dropping tunnel gate.

## Main Visual Highlight

The blue-cyan pixel-phase pass along the side of the oncoming train, followed by the purple-eyed look-back and gate trap.

## Why It Is Worth Producing

It has a strong first-second hook, a clean cause-and-effect chase line, and a visually distinctive escape mechanic that can read clearly in a short runtime.

## Whether It Should Be Split Into Multiple Videos

No. Produce as one short standalone clip.

## Series Name

Future Courier Escape Files

## Production Difficulty

High, because the shot continuity, train-bike spacing, and pixel-phase effect must remain readable without becoming teleportation.

## Main Generation Risks

- the train may appear in inconsistent positions
- the pixel-phase may look like teleportation
- the tunnel layout may flip across cuts
- the rider count may accidentally increase
- the chase may become overcut and unreadable

## Confirmed Direction

High-quality anime sci-fi action with strong spatial continuity inside one subway tunnel.

## Forbidden Direction

Random cyberpunk clutter, game UI overlays, extra riders, arbitrary magic effects, teleportation logic, or changing bike and costume designs.

## Final Decision

- Produce now
