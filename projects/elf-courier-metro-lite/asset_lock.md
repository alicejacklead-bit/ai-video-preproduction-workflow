# Asset Lock

## Characters

### Heroine

- identity: young female elf courier
- face: refined anime facial structure, slender jawline
- ears: long pointed elf ears, always visible when angle allows
- hair: silver-white long high ponytail, no style changes
- eyes: glowing purple irises, especially important in close shots
- outfit: black-silver cropped future rider jacket
- accent lights: cyan-blue glowing linework on jacket
- lower garment: black tactical skirt-style riding bottom
- footwear: dark long boots
- movement style: precise, aggressive, controlled, never clumsy
- forbidden changes: no hair color shift, no ponytail removal, no costume redesign, no random armor additions

### Pursuer

- identity: adult male rider
- head: bald
- mask: black half-face respirator mask
- outfit: dark gray-black riding suit
- movement style: forceful, heavy, intimidating
- forbidden changes: no helmet replacement, no face exposure in random shots, no outfit recolor

## Vehicles

### Heroine Bike

- type: future sport motorcycle
- color: deep blue primary body
- accent lines: cyan-blue glowing linework
- silhouette: streamlined, fast, athletic
- continuity rule: same body shape and color in every shot
- forbidden changes: no wheel design swap, no matte-black recolor, no transformation sequence

### Pursuer Bike

- type: heavy sport motorcycle
- color: matte black
- silhouette: bulkier and more aggressive than heroine bike
- continuity rule: same heavy massing and matte finish in every shot
- forbidden changes: no blue accents, no major body reshaping

## Environment

- location: one dark, old subway tunnel only
- surfaces: damp concrete, grime, real graffiti, wet reflections
- lighting: cyan-blue neon accents, practical maintenance glows, strong warm-yellow train headlight
- atmosphere: air particles, mist, wheel sparks when braking, motion blur
- forbidden additions: no station concourse, no gas station, no daylight, no exterior city, no rocket hardware

## Key Props and Hazards

### Subway Train

- body: old metal subway train
- front light: bright warm yellow headlights
- motion: fast oncoming movement
- continuity rule: physically occupies the tunnel and must feel heavy and dangerous
- forbidden changes: no bullet-train redesign, no transparent body, no impossible path shift

### Safety Gate

- type: industrial tunnel barrier
- motion: drops vertically with force
- position: beyond the train, on the heroine's cleared side, blocking the pursuer's path
- forbidden changes: no sci-fi shield wall, no explosion trigger, no side-opening door behavior

## Effect Lock

- effect: short cyan-blue pixel-phase effect only at the pass moment
- function: reduces collision profile visually while preserving physical path along the train side gap
- rule: effect must read as controlled phase-skimming, not teleportation, not magical portal travel
- forbidden changes: no purple magic aura, no explosion burst, no full-body disappearance across space
