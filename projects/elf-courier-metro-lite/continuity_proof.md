# Continuity Proof

## Goal

Validate the three riskiest continuity points before full generation.

## Proof Shot 1: Master Chase Geography

- camera: CAM 1
- what to test: heroine in front, pursuer behind, same tunnel layout, same bike colors, stable chase axis
- must pass:
  - one tunnel only
  - heroine deep-blue bike remains visually distinct
  - pursuer matte-black bike stays behind
  - wet graffiti tunnel reads consistently

## Proof Shot 2: Train Threat and Decision

- camera: CAM 3 plus CAM 4 inserts
- what to test: oncoming train clearly ahead, heroine choosing not to brake, glowing purple eye and handlebar control reading cleanly
- must pass:
  - train headlight direction is clear
  - heroine remains on same path
  - eye color, hair, and outfit remain locked
  - no random extra props or riders appear

## Proof Shot 3: Side-Gap Pass and Failed Follow

- camera: CAM 5 into CAM 6
- what to test: the heroine phase-skims through the narrow side gap beside the train; the pursuer cannot copy it and must drift-stop
- must pass:
  - pass path is visible as train exterior plus wall-side clearance
  - effect reads as short cyan-blue pixel-phase, not teleportation
  - pursuer remains on original side and fails physically
  - sparks and brake motion are strong but readable

## Pass Criteria

- room and route continuity stay clear
- no identity drift
- no bike redesign
- no extra riders
- no station switch
- no magical portal behavior

## Proof Result

Pending generation

## If Partial Pass or Fail

Revise only the smallest upstream module first:

1. tighten the relevant prompt segment
2. restate the physical path in CAM 5
3. simplify any overloaded action beat

Do not rebuild the full concept unless the geography itself breaks.
