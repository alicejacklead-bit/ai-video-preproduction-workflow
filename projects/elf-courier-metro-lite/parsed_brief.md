# Parsed Brief

## Project Goal

Produce a 15-second anime sci-fi chase short for Douyin with strong first-second hook, clear spatial logic, and one memorable reversal.

## Video Type

Narrative short / anime action chase

## Platform

Douyin

## Duration

15 seconds

## Aspect Ratio

4:3

## Language

No spoken dialogue required

## Target Audience

Short-form viewers who enjoy high-intensity anime action and sci-fi chase scenes.

## Core Hook

A female elf courier is already at high speed inside a dark subway tunnel while a masked rider rapidly closes in and an oncoming train suddenly appears ahead.

## Core Conflict

She seems trapped between the pursuer behind and the train ahead.

## Ending

She phase-pixelates through the narrow side gap beside the train, the pursuer fails to follow, and she drops a gate to cut him off.

## Main Characters

- female elf courier
- masked male pursuer

## Main Scene

One damp, graffiti-covered old subway tunnel with cyan-blue neon accents and warm train headlights.

## Visual Style

High-quality anime sci-fi action, dark gritty tunnel textures, wet walls, neon graffiti, strong cyan-vs-warm-light contrast, kinetic low-angle tracking, dynamic blur, sparks, and airborne particles.

## Required Elements

- low-angle motorcycle chase opening
- rear-view mirror threat beat
- oncoming train reveal
- eye and hand insert
- pixel-phase side-gap pass
- pursuer drift brake with sparks
- heroine look-back on far side
- safety gate slam ending

## Forbidden Elements

- gas station
- rocket
- technology grenade
- third rider
- hair or costume changes
- motorcycle color or model changes
- bike abandonment
- game UI
- gore
- subtitles
- meaningless magic VFX
- literal travel through solid train body
- unexplained teleportation
- excessive chaotic cutting

## Production Constraints

- one tunnel only
- clear geography across the full chase
- the heroine must remain on bike for the full action sequence
- the pursuer must remain behind her until the failed follow attempt
- the train and gate must read as physical hazards inside the same space

## Main Generation Risks

- unreadable tunnel geography
- effect overload during the phase pass
- the model inventing extra riders or props
- inconsistent train position
- cutting too quickly to understand the cause-and-effect chain
