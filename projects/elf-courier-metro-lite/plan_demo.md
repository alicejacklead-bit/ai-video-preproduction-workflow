# Plan Demo

## Project Information

- Duration: 15 seconds
- Aspect Ratio: 4:3
- Style: high-quality anime sci-fi action
- Main Characters: female elf courier, masked male pursuer
- Main Scene: one dark old subway tunnel with neon graffiti and wet concrete
- Language: no dialogue, action-led
- Output Mode: Lite Mode

## One-Sentence Story

A hunted elf courier lures her pursuer toward an oncoming subway train, phase-rides through the narrow side gap beside it, and seals the rider behind a dropping tunnel gate.

## Character Relationships

- The heroine is the hunted but tactically superior courier.
- The pursuer is aggressive, close, and physically dangerous, but less prepared.

## Scene Layout

- Single straight tunnel segment with a slight visual bend in the far distance
- Heroine rides on the right half of the track corridor
- Pursuer stays behind on the same chase line
- Oncoming train appears ahead on the center-left track mass, its headlight filling the tunnel
- Narrow escape path exists along the train side and tunnel wall on the heroine's route
- Safety gate is mounted beyond the train on the heroine's side of the tunnel

## Main Visual Hook

The heroine appears trapped between a fast-closing pursuer and an oncoming train, but uses a pixel-phase side pass instead of braking.

## Timeline

### 0.0-3.0 Seconds | Hook

Visual: low-angle tracking behind and slightly below the heroine bike; the pursuer rapidly closes the gap in the same tunnel
Action: both bikes race at full speed through wet railside concrete and neon graffiti reflections
Camera: chase cam low to the ground, then a brief mirror insert
Expression: the heroine stays focused; the pursuer is intent and predatory
Dialogue or Audio: engine roar, tunnel reverb, tire hiss, metal rattle
Information Given to Audience: the heroine is in danger, being chased fast, and cannot simply slow down

### 3.0-7.0 Seconds | Conflict

Visual: frontward cut reveals a blinding yellow train headlight rushing toward them; close insert on the heroine's glowing purple eye and thumb hovering near the handlebar control
Action: the heroine deliberately holds course instead of braking
Camera: forward-facing reveal, then eye insert, then handlebar insert
Expression: controlled resolve rather than panic
Dialogue or Audio: rising rail thunder, sharper engine pitch, faint electric phase charge
Information Given to Audience: the tunnel is about to close into a fatal trap, but the heroine has a plan

### 7.0-11.5 Seconds | Escalation

Visual: just before impact, the heroine and bike break into cyan-blue pixel fragments along their silhouette and skim through the narrow side clearance between the train exterior and tunnel wall
Action: she phase-rides through the gap while maintaining forward momentum; the pursuer reaches the same point too late and cannot fit or replicate the move
Camera: side-tracking geography shot first, then a tighter dynamic pass shot
Expression: the heroine remains locked in; the pursuer jolts into emergency reaction
Dialogue or Audio: electric pixel crackle, train rush, sparks, brake scream
Information Given to Audience: this is not teleportation, but a precisely timed side-gap pass enabled by her pixel-phase ability

### 11.5-15.0 Seconds | Reversal or Ending

Visual: the heroine emerges on the far side, glances back with glowing purple eyes, presses the handlebar button, and a heavy safety gate slams down in front of the drifting pursuer
Action: the pursuer throws the bike into a desperate spark-heavy drift stop as the gate drops and blocks him
Camera: medium rear three-quarter on the heroine, cut to gate drop impact, end on blocked pursuer
Expression: calm victory from the heroine; frustrated forced stop from the pursuer
Dialogue or Audio: gate alarm chirp, hard metal slam, fading engine rev
Information Given to Audience: the heroine has fully outplayed and cut off the chase
