# Confirmed Decisions

- Output mode: Lite Mode
- Platform: Douyin
- Duration: 15 seconds
- Aspect ratio: 4:3
- Story format: one continuous chase in one subway tunnel
- Core reversal: the heroine does not pass through solid train mass; she phase-pixelates and rides through the narrow side clearance between the train exterior and tunnel wall
- Ending: safety gate falls in front of the pursuer after the heroine clears the train

# Locked Characters

- Heroine: young female elf, silver-white high ponytail, long pointed elf ears, glowing purple eyes, black-silver cropped future rider jacket with cyan glowing lines, black tactical skirted lower garment, dark long boots
- Pursuer: adult bald male, black half-face respirator mask, dark gray-black riding suit

# Locked Scenes

- One old subway tunnel only
- Wet walls, real graffiti, worn concrete, rail bed, maintenance lights, damp mist, cyan-blue neon accents
- No new locations, no platform, no station hall, no surface street

# Locked Products or Props

- Heroine bike: deep-blue future sport motorcycle with cyan glowing linework
- Pursuer bike: matte-black heavy sport motorcycle
- Oncoming train: old metal subway train with bright warm yellow headlights
- Final gate: industrial tunnel safety gate that drops vertically

# Approved Camera Plan

- Same forward chase axis throughout the clip
- Wide chase geography first, then inserts, then reversal, then payoff
- The train always approaches from ahead on the heroine's path
- The pursuer always remains behind the heroine until blocked

# Approved Story Structure

- Hook: chase and closing threat
- Escalation: oncoming train appears and heroine commits
- Reversal: pixel-phase side pass
- Payoff: pursuer stops, heroine traps him

# Approved Output Mode

- Lite Mode

# User Corrections

- No gas station
- No rocket
- No tech grenade
- No third rider
- No hair, outfit, or bike color change
- No bike abandonment
- No game interface, gore, subtitles, or meaningless magic effects
- No literal train-body pass-through
- No unreadable teleportation

# Reusable Rules

- When the escape mechanic is unusual, state its physical path repeatedly in both plan and prompt
- In short chase videos, wide geography must arrive before effect-heavy inserts

# Rejected Directions

- Overcut music-video logic
- Random fantasy spell visuals
- Incoherent tunnel switching
