# Video Prompt

## Global Settings

Duration: 15 seconds
Aspect Ratio: 4:3
Visual Style: high-quality Japanese anime sci-fi action animation
Camera Texture: kinetic tracking shots, low-angle speed coverage, cinematic motion blur, readable action geography
Lighting: dark wet subway tunnel with cyan-blue neon accents and strong warm-yellow oncoming train headlights
Character Consistency: locked heroine and pursuer design across all shots
Scene Consistency: one old graffiti-covered subway tunnel only
Product Consistency: same deep-blue heroine bike, same matte-black pursuer bike, same old metal subway train, same tunnel gate

## Locked Characters and Assets

- Heroine: young female elf courier, silver-white long high ponytail, long pointed elf ears, glowing purple eyes, black-silver cropped future rider jacket with cyan glowing lines, black tactical skirt-style lower garment, dark long boots, precise and fearless riding posture
- Heroine bike: deep-blue streamlined future sport motorcycle with cyan glowing linework, same body shape in every shot
- Pursuer: adult bald male rider, black half-face respirator mask, dark gray-black riding suit, aggressive body language
- Pursuer bike: matte-black heavy sport motorcycle, bulkier than the heroine bike
- Train: old metal subway train with bright warm-yellow headlights, fast oncoming motion
- Environment: one dark old subway tunnel, wet concrete, grime, neon graffiti, damp mist, rail reflections, airborne dust, occasional wheel sparks

## Timeline

### 0.0-3.0 Seconds

Scene: one dark subway tunnel
Character Position: heroine in front on the right half of the tunnel path, pursuer behind closing the gap on the same chase axis
Action: low-angle high-speed motorcycle chase, tunnel walls rushing by, rear-view mirror briefly shows the masked rider getting closer
Expression: heroine focused and controlled, pursuer intent and threatening
Shot Size: moving medium-wide with one mirror insert
Camera Position: low behind the heroine bike, then near mirror line
Camera Movement: fast forward tracking, brief insert vibration
Dialogue: none
Sound: roaring engines, tire hiss, tunnel metal echo

### 3.0-7.0 Seconds

Scene: same tunnel, no location change
Character Position: heroine still leading, pursuer still behind, oncoming train now appears directly ahead
Action: forward reveal of a fast incoming subway train with blinding warm headlights; heroine does not brake; tight insert of her glowing purple eye and thumb nearing the handlebar button
Expression: calm tactical resolve, not panic
Shot Size: wide frontal reveal followed by extreme close inserts
Camera Position: ahead on the chase line, then tight on eye and hand
Camera Movement: rapid pullback on the reveal, minimal movement on inserts
Dialogue: none
Sound: rail thunder builds, engine pitch rises, faint electric charge tone

### 7.0-11.5 Seconds

Scene: same tunnel, train fills the path
Character Position: heroine reaches the near-collision point first; pursuer arrives behind her too late
Action: at the last moment, the heroine and her deep-blue bike briefly break into controlled cyan-blue pixel fragments along their silhouette and skim through the narrow physical side gap between the outside of the moving train and the tunnel wall; she continues forward with momentum; the pursuer cannot fit and cannot replicate the move
Expression: heroine locked in concentration; pursuer shocked into emergency reaction
Shot Size: readable side geography wide, then tighter action pass
Camera Position: side-tracking parallel to the train exterior, then low near the pursuer braking zone
Camera Movement: fast lateral follow for the heroine pass, whip toward pursuer drift-stop
Dialogue: none
Sound: electric pixel crackle, violent train rush, tire scream, sparks

### 11.5-15.0 Seconds

Scene: far side of the train in the same tunnel
Character Position: heroine already clear on the other side, pursuer blocked before the pass point
Action: the heroine looks back over her shoulder, glowing purple eyes visible, presses the handlebar button, and a heavy industrial tunnel safety gate slams down in front of the drifting pursuer, fully cutting off the chase
Expression: heroine calm and victorious, pursuer frustrated and forced into a hard stop
Shot Size: medium rear three-quarter to close-up, then gate impact and blocked pursuer
Camera Position: beyond the train facing back along the chase path
Camera Movement: brief settle on heroine, impact cut to the gate
Dialogue: none
Sound: gate trigger chirp, massive metal slam, fading rev, echoing sparks

## Continuity Requirements

- one subway tunnel only
- the heroine is always ahead of the pursuer until he is trapped
- the train always comes from directly ahead
- the heroine never abandons the bike
- the pixel-phase pass must read as a narrow side-gap skim beside the train exterior, not a pass through the train body, not teleportation
- maintain consistent heroine hair, glowing purple eyes, jacket design, and bike color
- maintain consistent pursuer mask, bald head, dark riding suit, and matte-black heavy bike
- keep geography readable with a stable chase axis and restrained shot count

## Negative Requirements

- no gas station
- no rocket
- no technology grenade
- no third rider
- no hair color change
- no outfit redesign
- no bike color or model change
- no bike abandonment
- no game interface
- no subtitles
- no gore
- no meaningless magic aura
