# Adaptation Guide

Use the same workflow across categories, but change what gets locked first.

## Dialogue Scenes

Prioritize:
- who faces whom
- room geography
- eyelines
- reverse-shot consistency
- reaction timing

Common failure mode:
The room flips between cuts because the camera map was never fixed.

## Action Clips

Prioritize:
- pursuit axis
- obstacle placement
- entry and exit points
- stunt readability
- physical cause and effect

Common failure mode:
The sequence becomes flashy but spatially unreadable.

## Product Videos

Prioritize:
- hero product visibility
- feature order
- hand interactions
- clean backgrounds
- branding consistency

Common failure mode:
The product keeps changing shape, finish, or scale.

## UGC or Creator-Style Videos

Prioritize:
- speaker identity
- speaking beats
- prop placement
- text overlay zones
- platform ratio constraints

Common failure mode:
The clip looks polished but loses the authentic creator rhythm.

## Comedy or Twist Endings

Prioritize:
- early hook
- clean escalation
- reveal camera
- reaction coverage
- ending hold

Common failure mode:
The joke premise is clear, but the reveal is framed too weakly to land.
